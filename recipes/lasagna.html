Lasagna

Description

The classic Italian recipe that feeds the family and leaves everyone happy!

Ingredients

  • Meat
    • Sweet Italian sausage
    • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Oregano
    • Basil
    • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook sauage, ground beef, over medium heat
  2. Stir in tomato products
  3. Stir in seasonings
  4. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours
  5. Cook, drain, and rinse lasagna noodles
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and seasonings
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  8. In a baking dish, add a layer of your sauce, then noodles, then cheese mixture, then Mozzarella, and repeat this until you are satisfied
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  10. Cover dish with foil
  11. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
  12. Remove foil, and bake for an additional 25 minutes
  13. Rest the lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
  14. Slice into squares and serve!
