Scalloped Potatoes

Description

Scalloped potatoes are a delightfully cheesy, cozy, and comforting dish that is sure to impress your dinner guests! Of course, this dish could also be enjoyed alone because no one ever just has a single serving of this incredible dish!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (preferably Yukon)
  • Cream sauce
    • Flour
    • Butter
    • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Any of your favorites such as thyme, rosemary, parsley, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Thinly slice potatoes
  3. In a small sauce pan, melt butter and stir in flour
  4. Whisk in the milk and seasonings of your choice
  5. Cook sauce on low until smooth and boiling, stirring occasionally with a whisk
  6. Reduce heat and stir in 1 cup of the cheddar cheese
  7. PLace a half of the sliced potatoes in a lightly greased 1.5 quart casserole dish (8*8 or 9*9 will work)
  8. Pour half of cheese sauce over potatoes
  9. Repeat with second layer of potatoes and cheese sauce
  10. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese on top
  11. Bake uncovered for about 1 hour at 350 degrees
